## Next Milestone

### v2.4.x - Hosting Control and Monetization

Status: Planned

Goal: enable hosting providers to sell and control Redis usage as a hosted feature.

Planned scope:

- fuller reseller management
- quotas and limits by package or reseller
- monetization hooks for hosted plans
- deeper per-account usage history
- better alerting and optimization controls
