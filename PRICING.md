A **Single-Server License** includes:

- WHM functionality as it becomes available in supported release lines

---

## License Details

### Server-bound license

Each license is tied to one server.

For the current licensing model:

- one license covers one server
- no per-account fees
- no per-instance fees
- no expiry on the current one-time license

If you migrate servers, the licensed IP can be updated through your [UnderHost client area](https://customerpanel.ca).

### Updates

All licenses currently include lifetime updates for released versions.

### Support

Support is provided by email and through the UnderHost client area.

Typical response time is within 24 hours.

- Email: [support@underhost.com](mailto:support@underhost.com)
- Client area: [customerpanel.ca](https://customerpanel.ca)

## Future Pricing Direction

A monthly subscription option may be introduced in the future for advanced hosting and enterprise-oriented capabilities, such as:

- hosting package integration
- reseller controls
- advanced monitoring and alerting
- high availability and clustering

This would be an optional model and **not a replacement** for existing lifetime licenses.

### Early adopters

Customers who purchase under the current lifetime model retain their lifetime license and included updates.
